Falling Star Portends Disaster

Residents of Braylock Bay reported sightings of an ominous falling star late last night. Eyewitnesses describe a bright blue ball of fire fell from the clear sky, landing just beyond the Everdark forrest to the southwest.

Residents are preparing for the worst, believing that this rare cosmic event portends imminent disaster unleashed upon the region.
