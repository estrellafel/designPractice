BUD GREY BACK DACKFROM FROM COAST

Salt Lake Trick Bike Rider Now Heading Three Team Combination

Bud Grey, the Salt Lake boy who has has made a name for himself as a trick rider arrived in the city yesterday after a years absence on the Pacific coast. Grey is now at the head of a three-team combination doing fancy stunts and tricks on a bicycle. With him are Fravilo Fravlo, the girl who looped the loop in a ball at the Salt Palace a couple of seasons ago and Zarrow, a comedian trick rider.

Grey is well known locally and is one of the few trick riders who has performed at the saucer track. He first began trick t riding in this city about five years ago and is now at the top of his profession. Grey was around visiting friends yesterday and is now making arrangements to put his turn on in Salt Lake. From here he goes east, where he is booked ahead for the summer season.

FARISH STREET SATURDAY
NIGHT: Christmas time is here again. And what a really great pleasure tis for me to say ... A MERRY, MERRY CHRISTMAS TO EVERYBODY, and in saying it we acknowledge our gratitude first to the ALMIGHTY, and then to all those whose good wishes, backing, favors and support it has been ours to have down through the closing year ... We say it with the same depth and fervor to all those to whom it has been our opportunity to render a service, who we have given our backing, favors, and support during the closing year ... to the rich, the poor, the black, the white, the learned and the un-leamed, to everybody who has been with us during the year ... UP AND DOWN FAR ISH STREET ... To all and every body, here, there, and everywhere, in the mellow movements of my Fish and Beer, in the language and meaning of Rudyard Kipling, I wish to say: Call a truce, that, to our labors let us feast with friends and neighbors, and be mer ry as the custom of our caste; For if "faint and forced the laughter," and if sadness follow after, We are richer by one mocking Christmas past. Again, and again, and again, A Merry, Merry Christmas to every body.
